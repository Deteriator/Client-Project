Is my contribution tax-deductible?

The Alex House Project is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) public charity; therefore, your contribution may be tax-deductible. Please check with your tax adviser to find out more.

Is there a minimum donation?

No! The beauty of donations is that you are open give as much or as a little as you can. We do have specific price points to make deciding on how much you want to donate easier

Whom do I contact if I have a question or problem making an online donation

Please direct questions to “Donation inquiry” on our contact page

Can I donate clothes/used goods?

Absolutely! We will gladly accept and gently used/almost new goods. Contact us and we will set up a drop off time

Want to get involved in other ways?

We are always looking for new faces to join our family

Email services@alexhouseproject.com and drop us a message!

About

The Alex House Project (AHP) is a 501 (c3) nonprofit, peer-led social service and leadership development organization that supports pregnant and parenting young mothers who resides in economically depressed neighborhoods in New York City.
